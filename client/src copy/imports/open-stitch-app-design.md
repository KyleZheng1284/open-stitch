Prompt: > Design a multi-screen, high-fidelity web application for "Open Stitch," an AI video editor. The overall aesthetic must be "Modern Dark Mode Pro-SaaS," utilizing charcoal backgrounds (#121212), deep slate surfaces, vibrant electric purple (#7F00FF) accents, and clean, readable Inter typography.

Screen 1: Landing Page (Intro)

A centered, professional introduction screen.

Logo/Title: The name "Open Stitch" in large, bold, professional sans-serif typography at the top-center.

Tagline/Description: Directly beneath, add a sub-headline: "The AI Video Assistant that turns raw footage into polished stories." Follow with a brief description paragraph: "Open Stitch analyzes your videos, helps you organize them, and uses conversational AI to understand the mood, style, and structure you want. Upload, reorganize, and let our agents handle the editing flow."

Main Action: Include a primary, filled-button labeled "Log In to Begin."

Visuals: Subtle background texture of abstract geometric patterns or a dark, slow-moving bokeh effect.

Screen 2: Dedicated Authentication (Google Login)

This screen appears after clicking "Log In to Begin."

Use a "glassmorphism" card centered in the viewport with soft glowing purple shadows.

Card Title: "Sign In to Open Stitch"

Sub-text: "To access and edit your videos, please log in with your Google account."

Main Button: A prominent button labeled "Continue with Google" with the official Google color 'G' logo.

Screen 3: Dedicated Video Upload (Workspace State)

A clean, focused layout with a single primary focus.

Center Workspace: A massive, dash-bordered upload zone occupying the middle of the screen.

Icons/Text: Clear visual cues: "Drag & Drop Your Videos Here" and a "Cloud Upload" icon. Include a "Browse Files" button.

Instruction: This screen has absolutely no sidebars, chat interfaces, or navigation menus. It is entirely locked to the file upload task.

Include progress bar elements for uploaded files to visualize the upload queue.

Screen 4: Assembly & AI Video Assistant (Reorganize State)

Center Workspace: Display the uploaded videos as draggable, reorderable thumbnail cards in a horizontal grid or timeline arrangement. Add a clear "Add More Clips" button.

Right Sidebar: Introduce the "AI Video Assistant" chatbot. It should have a clean, conversational UI with a sleek avatar.

Functionality: The chat initiates with qualifying questions (e.g., "What is the primary mood of this video?" "Do you want background music?").

Include a "Finalize & Stitch" primary action button at the bottom of the sidebar.

Screen 5: Backend Visualization (Progress State)

A monitoring dashboard showing backend progress.

Use a horizontal graph with connected nodes inspired by n8n.

Nodes: Label them "Scene Detection," "Script Generation," "Color Tuning," and "Final Render."

Show status indicators (Loading/Success) on each node as the process simulates completion.
